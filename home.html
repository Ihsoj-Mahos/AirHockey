Score the required number of goals by striking the puck into the opponent's goal

Move the paddle by pointing the cursor in the direction you want to move

You cannot cross the half line with your paddle

Make sure to not score an own goal :)

Arena

About the game

Air Hockey is a popular arcade game (perhaps the favourite of a lot of us). We have tried to translate the flavour of this game into the virtual world.

This game was developed as a part of the GameDev Hackathon 2022 organised by Devcom, a division of WnCC(Web and Coding Club) at IIT Bombay.

The programming languages used here are HTML5, CSS and Javascript. W3Schools and StackOverflow have been very useful resources in the development process.

About the Developers

This game was created by Soham Joshi, first year undergraduate at the Computer Science Department and Shubhranil Chatterjee, first year undergraduate at the Mechanical Engineering Department at IIT Bombay.
